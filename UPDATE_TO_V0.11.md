# RoundWise v0.11

- Removed in-app celebration animations.
- Added 1-second full-screen score posters between holes.
- Poster mapping:
  - score of 8 = Snowman
  - -3 or better to par = Albatross
  - -2 = Eagle
  - -1 = Birdie
  - Even par = Par
  - +1 = Bogey
  - +2 = Double Bogey
  - +3 or worse = Meltdown
- Includes branded RoundWise posters provided by the user.
- No Supabase migration required.
